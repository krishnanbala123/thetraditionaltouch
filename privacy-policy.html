Shipping Policy

Dispatch Time

All our products are made by order and will have minimum dispatch time of 5 working days from the date of order.In certain case, Dispatch might take more than 5 working days which will be prior informed in description of product, so please check before placing your urgent orders.


Shipping Time

• Tamil Nadu: Usually Delivered within 1 - 2 working days after dispatch of product.

• Other States: Delivery may take 3 - 5 working days after dispatch, it depends upon your location.

We ship through ST courier and DTDC.

Tracking Information

Once your order is dispatched tracking will be updated in your order profile. You can use this to monitor the live shipment of your product.

• Order data: items purchased, order history, and payment confirmation status (we do not store card details).

• Usage data: pages visited, browser type, device info, and IP address via server logs.

• Cookies: session cookies for cart/authentication and optional analytics cookies.

How We Use Your Information

• To process and fulfil your orders and send order/dispatch confirmation emails.

• To improve site performance through aggregated analytics.

Third-Party Services
